**What's this?**


This is an xposed module.


The main functions include one-key commands and automatic commands, similar to ColorOS's Xiaobu commands, or MIUI's automatic tasks, or automatic task applications such as Tasker.

Compared with other similar apps, ShortX provides more system-level triggers and operations.
